SafeHeat Services attended the property after the tenant reported no heating and no hot water.

The engineer identified low boiler pressure and a faulty valve. The valve was replaced during the visit and the system pressure was restored.

The repair was marked as completed. A follow-up check was recommended if the tenant reports further loss of pressure.
